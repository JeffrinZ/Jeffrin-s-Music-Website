Jeffrin John

Singer/Songwriter

Jeffrin John is an Indian artist who resides in America. He is a musician, singer and songwriter (2012-present). He has a great passion for music and singing. In the late autumn of 2012, he started his musical journey by setting up his own YouTube channel. He has given numerous concerts and live performances. He has also been awarded with different awards and has been recognized in various competitions like outstanding instrumental competition, interstate singing competition and much more back in the days in high school. He has also been recognized for his amazing talent in playing Piano/Keyboard and guitar. He is also a recording artist, proficient in mixing & mastering.
